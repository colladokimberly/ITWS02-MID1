About Marinette

Marinette seems to be a normal teenage girl, she dreams of becoming a fashion designer, is a bit awkward and has a crush on a classmate. But she has a secret other teenagers don't have, she lives a double life as a crime-fighting superhero known as Ladybug.

Marinette's Characteristics

  1. She's a sweet girl
  2. She's a joyful girl
  3. She's a Hero

Marinette's Lover/Partner

Adrien Agreste

Marinette and Adrien's love story in "Miraculous Ladybug" is a captivating blend of secret crushes, superheroic partnerships, and the complexities of hidden identities.
